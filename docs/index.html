Out of this World Advice

awakening.io

Out of This World Advice is an interactive musical installation.

Ambient sound and light movement attracts you to a monolith. Upon approaching you notice the rectlinear shape towards the top of the structure where the light appears to be coming from.

They look like buttons, you push them and seem to have activated it. You hear a rhythmic, musical sound. Pressing another button activates another sound.

More sounds are activated until the first level is full, the lights seem a call to action towards the next level of buttons...

OWA at Chillits 2018

Our vision for OWA...allows for musical expression and creation, and it requires human interaction and collaboration.

A possible future form.
